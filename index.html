Our vision of a new world

Technology has been developing a great deal since a few years ago. As such, new techniques and features have been built around an almost endless supply of mobiles, computers, tablets and other devices design for our comfort.

 

New and exciting features are create for the newer and most tech devices, but, what about old and not so powerful devices? Even though must people now have the budget to get a new pone or tablet, or even a computer every couple of years, some can’t.

 

And some technology, such as Virtual Reality, has been there for a lot of time, but not all the people have their own headset or even try such an amazing and innovative creation. So much people that can’t afford to buy their own, as some of them can be as expensive as 300 dollars. So, is there and alternative for these people?

 

Well, kind of.

V-Right Now was create for the purpose of giving people the opportunity to make their own VR headsets in the most affordable materials. Our investigations has said that the cheapest one could be make with a single dollar!

 

Our main objective is to give the people what the people have made. We believe technology should not be in the hands of a few but into the whole mass. Those who make it should willingly give other, if maybe not the best of their creation, a type of indication on how to get something as affordable as possible.

If you can do, improve, or make something more attractive, then do it!

And if you wish to help us with our mission, in translations, making new themes or even improving it with your knowledge, please, message us into our contact form.
